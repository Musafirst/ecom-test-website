Use:


* Use only as subtle background texture.
* Never place heavy text on top of strong pattern.
* Keep opacity low when used as overlay.
* Do not crop the pattern in a way that looks random.
* Do not recolor the pattern.

```css

  background-size: 420px auto;
```
