# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Fragrance enthusiasts who know their oud from their amber. They actively seek niche, affordable luxury brands and compare performance and longevity. They land on Jamm Trade with discernment — they don't need to be sold, they need to be convinced the curation is trustworthy and the selection is worth their time.

## Product Purpose

Jamm Trade is a curated fragrance and lifestyle e-commerce store. Primary focus: perfume collections (oud, amber, daily). Secondary: electronics and upcoming verticals (Jamm Fleet — car rentals, Jamm Cargo — shipping services). The store's value is its curation and taste, not price competition. Success looks like a customer who trusts the store's eye enough to buy without researching elsewhere.

## Brand Personality

Bold, curated, aspirational. The brand has conviction about what it stocks. It doesn't hedge or over-explain. It presents products with quiet authority.

## Anti-references

- Generic Shopify stores: templated grid layouts, blue CTAs, zero editorial voice
- Loud perfume retailer sites: discount-first, cluttered, promotion-overloaded
- Ultra-dark neon luxury: black + neon gold, crypto/NFT aesthetic, cold and aggressive

## Design Principles

1. **Curation over catalog** — every surface should feel like a considered edit, not a product dump. Fewer things presented with more confidence.
2. **Authority without intimidation** — premium but not cold. The customer should feel like they've been let in, not shut out.
3. **Let materials speak** — product imagery and fragrance photography should dominate. The UI steps back and frames, it doesn't compete.
4. **Coherence across verticals** — Fleet, Cargo, and Shop share a brand world. The design language must stretch without fracturing.
5. **No decoration for its own sake** — every motion, border, and glow must earn its place. Restraint is the differentiator.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Reduced motion respected via `prefers-reduced-motion`. Color contrast checked for gold-on-dark and cream-on-dark pairings.
